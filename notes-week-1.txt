7 main principles (from gestalt principles)
all are connected and are guidelines
use these to guide the user through the UI

* Alignemnt
  grids are a tool for Alignemnt
  setting relationships from one element ot the next
* Emphasis
  creates interest and pulls the users attention to a focal point
* Hierarchy
  the relationships between elements in a composition(app)
* Consistancy
  similar items should look similar
* White space
  the unmarked space between elements
  must relate to the other element
* Balance
  optical equilibrium - symetrical and asymetrical
* Unity
  all elements are in agreement
